5 july 2020

I've been a musician since the day I was born. Both of my parents are musicians, although very different kinds. My mom is a choir director and a classically trained soprano and pianist. My dad loves post-punk and riffs. So I grew up singing classically, but in the last 5 years I've been lucky enough to tour the entire United States in a punk/metal band called Mocklove.







I've been so incredibly lucky to meet some of my best friends (here is a Good One™, his name is Ken and tours the entire world).





And I even met my incredible partner while both his band and my band were on tour. We played the same show.




(That's him up there. He may look scary but he's the sweetest most gentle person in the world.)


My music career has led to some crazy stuff! Outside of touring, I compose, produce, and engineer music, as well as consult for an audio-branding company based out of the UK. Well, at least I was consulting BEFORE the pandemic.
Here is a very awesome gig I accidentally stumbled into -- Giorgio Armani used one of my pop songs in 2017 for their Fall/Winter ad campaign.
My music was heard on New York and Paris Fashion Week runways.





Other than that, my musical direction and compositions have been used for commercial purposes by many large and small businesses across Ohio, Pennsylvania, and now St. Louis!


Now you know a little bit about my background and some of the cool stuff I've done. Upwards and onwards! Fight the good fight!
